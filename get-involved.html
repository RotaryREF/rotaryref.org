Get Involved

Join the movement to restore India's ecosystems, revive water bodies, and create sustainable livelihoods through large-scale afforestation and community-based environmental projects.

How You Can Help

park

1. Plant a Tree

Sponsor a sapling, grove, or an acre of forest. Each contribution supports planting, nurturing, and local livelihood creation.

₹ XX – one sapling | ₹ XX – mini grove | ₹XX – community grove

groups

2. Volunteer

Join our plantation drives, school programs, or field visits. Help track survival rates, engage students, and spread awareness in your community.

handshake

3. Partner through CSR

Collaborate on large-scale, auditable, and community-owned projects. REF ensures transparency, environmental compliance, and credible impact reporting.

business

4. Club Participation

Rotary clubs can adopt REF projects, replicate models in their region, or link with local NGOs and governments.

school

5. Rotaract, Youth & School Engagement

Bring REF to your Rotaract Club, campus — conduct "Plant & Protect" sessions, tree drives, and eco-literacy activities that count towards Rotary's Green credits.
