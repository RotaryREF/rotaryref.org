Rooting for a Green and Gorgeous Planet

The Rotary Environment Foundation (REF) is a Rotary-led initiative dedicated to restoring India's ecosystems, reviving water bodies, and creating sustainable livelihoods through large-scale afforestation and community-based environmental projects.

Founded on Rotary's belief in Service Above Self, REF connects citizens, corporates, and communities to build a greener tomorrow — one sapling, one lake, one village at a time.

Our Impact

5 million+

Saplings planted across India under the Rotary Koti Nati mission

1,500+

Acres of afforestation in Challakere, Karnataka — one of India's largest Rotary ecological projects

100+

Acres reclaimed at Kolar Gold Fields, transforming toxic dumps into healthy green zones

8,000+

Rural livelihoods supported through fruit, spice, and medicinal tree plantations

Three

Lakes revived after 40 years of drought in Challakere

Our Initiatives

Beyond planting trees, we are committed to a holistic approach to environmental conservation through various targeted programs.

forest

Tree Plantation

Spearheading mass afforestation drives to restore ecological balance and create green cover.

water_drop

Water Conservation

Reviving traditional water bodies and promoting rainwater harvesting to ensure water security.

school

Community Awareness

Educating communities and schools about sustainable practices for a lasting impact.

Our Vision

A greener, healthier, and more sustainable India where every community contributes to environmental restoration.

Our Mission

To drive Rotary's environmental focus area through afforestation, water and soil conservation, pollution mitigation, and nature-based livelihoods — in partnership with local communities, schools, and CSR allies.
